Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Gully Cricket Scorecard ("the app") is developed by Vigneshwaran. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

No Data Collection

Gully Cricket Scorecard does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All match data, scores, and statistics you create are stored only on your own device.

No Internet Access Required

The app works fully offline. It does not send any data to our servers or any third party, because we do not operate any servers that receive your data.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, the app does not knowingly collect information from children or anyone else.
